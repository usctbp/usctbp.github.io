Why Join Tau Beta Pi?

What distinguishes Tau Beta Pi from other honor societies?

If you are reading this, you have most likely been asked to join Tau Beta Pi and are wondering why choose this organization among other honor programs. Being one of the oldest honor societies in the United States and upholding strict academic standards, entrance into TBP gets you recognition from the faculty here at USC and other universities, as well as that of professionals in industry.

As part of Tau Beta Pi, you will often find yourself with unique opportunities to connect with recruiters from the top corporations, or will gain privileged access to networking opportunities organized by both Viterbi and TBP. More importantly, it is a great opportunity to connect with like-minded people, and forge long lasting bonds with engineers across the full spectrum of disciplines.

To that end, TBP strives to create events that allow us to get to know one another and often give back to the community at the same time. If anything else, we also tend to give free pizza; think about it.

Member Benefits

High-Quality Networking

Meet, socialize, and build your network with your most exemplary collegues and with industry professionals at our social events.

Resume Book

Your resume will be added to our resume book, referenced by recuiters looking for the very best candidates. It's an excellent and exclusive way to build your reputation and jump start your career.

Graduation Stole

Attend Tau Beta Pi events to earn points that will allow you to wear a special stole during graduation, distinguishing your hard work and excellent character.

Free Food

We hold many events throughout the year where you can fill up on free food like pizza, pie, cookies, and more.
