The oldest and largest engineering
honors society on USC campus.

Welcome to USC's Tau Beta Pi chapter, part of the second oldest honor society in the United States and the oldest dedicated to engineering. We are established to connect with and honor those who represent USC with their exemplary scholarship and distinguished character and whom we expect to leave a meaningful mark on the engineering profession. We hold fun events and provide excellent networking opportunities to our members.

Initiation to Tau Beta Pi is by invitation only.
